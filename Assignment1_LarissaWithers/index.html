Chickpea Curry

I love trying new things and making vegetarian dishes so when I found this recipe, I knew I just had to try it. Its super simple and easy to make and packed with flavour and healthy ingredients. This is also great for meal prep, it makes a lot so that it will last you throughout the week. I love to pair it with rice but can also be served with quinoa or naan bread.

  • -½ cup diced onions
  • -1 clove garlic minced
  • -1 tablespoon ginger grated
  • -1 tablespoon curry powder
  • -1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • -¼ teaspoon ground cumin
  • -¼ teaspoon paprika
  • -¼ teaspoon chili powder optional
  • -1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • -1 ½ cups diced tomatoes
  • -1 cup canned full fat coconut milk
  • -Sea salt and pepper to taste
  • -2 handfuls of fresh baby spinach
  • 1. Heat olive oil in the frying pan than sauté the onions on medium heat. Once the onions are soft add the garlic and ginger and let them cook for a minute.
  • 2. Add in all the seasonings (curry powder, coriander, cumin, paprika, and chili) and stir for 30 seconds.
  • 3. Next pour in the coconut milk, chickpeas, and diced tomatoes. Cook for 5 minutes once it comes to a boil, stirring often.
  • 4. Toss in some salt and pepper to taste and add spinach. Cook for another minute.
  • 5. Serve with rice, quinoa, or naan bread.
